About

Bridging Policy & Industry in Digital Assets

A first-generation policy expert who has participated in the institutionalization of Korea's digital asset industry. She began her career at a cryptocurrency exchange in 2015, gaining frontline experience in one of the sector's core market infrastructures during its formative years.

She took part in the founding of the Korea Blockchain Association, where she was involved in early discussions on regulatory frameworks and the policy direction for digital assets. She also served as Chair of the Exchange Committee within the Virtual Asset Policy Task Force, facilitating working-level dialogue between industry participants and policymakers.

Building on this combined industry and policy experience, she founded the International Digital Asset Policy Institute (IDAPI) to help bridge global standards with Korea's regulatory environment and support coherent policy development.
