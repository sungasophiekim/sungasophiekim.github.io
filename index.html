About

Shaping the Future of Digital Assets in Korea

A pioneering figure in Korea's crypto industry with a proven track record as a successful exited founder. Since entering the cryptocurrency sector at Korea's first Bitcoin exchange in 2015, Sunga Sophie Kim has been at the forefront of blockchain innovation and advocacy in Asia.

As the first and only female CEO among 200+ Korean crypto exchanges, she founded Hanbitco — one of the first exchanges in Korea to obtain a Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) license from the Korean government in 2021.

She has played a significant role in shaping Korea's blockchain regulatory framework as Chairwoman of the Exchange Committee at the Korea Blockchain Association, and continues to drive the industry forward as a Web3 investor.
